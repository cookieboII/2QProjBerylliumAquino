Genderfluidity

Gender-fluid people are individuals whose gender is not fixed. By not fixed, I mean their gender changes over time. Sometimes every day, sometimes every month. They might identify as any identity in the gender spectrum and beyond.

They might or might not change their gender expression or pronouns when their identity change.
